# ES Frameworks

## 志望動機

Use this order:

1. First sentence: state the reason for applying.
2. Applicant background: connect personal experience, study, research, internship, project, or values.
3. Company fit: point to business, role, product, technology, customers, mission, or working style.
4. Future contribution: explain how the applicant will contribute after joining.

Avoid:

- 「貴社の理念に共感しました」without evidence.
- Only saying the company is famous, global, stable, or has good training.
- A company paragraph that could be reused for competitors.

Template:

```text
私は、[自分の経験・関心]を通じて、[実現したいこと]に強い関心を持つようになりました。
その中で、[会社の具体的な事業・強み]を通じて[社会・顧客・技術への価値]を生み出している貴社に魅力を感じています。
入社後は、[自分の強み・専門性]を活かし、[職種・事業での貢献]に取り組みたいです。
```

## 自己PR

Use this order:

1. Strength in one sentence.
2. Situation where the strength was needed.
3. Specific action taken by the applicant.
4. Result and learning.
5. How the strength will be used at the company.

Good strengths for ES:

- 課題を構造化して粘り強く改善する力
- 周囲を巻き込みながら物事を前に進める力
- 未知の分野でも自ら学び成果につなげる力
- 相手の立場を踏まえて調整する力

## ガクチカ

Use STAR, but write it as natural ES prose:

- Situation: what was the context?
- Task: what was difficult or required?
- Action: what did the applicant do specifically?
- Result: what changed?
- Learning: what will the applicant carry forward?

Prefer concrete verbs:

- 分析した
- 仮説を立てた
- 優先順位を整理した
- 関係者に働きかけた
- 改善策を実行した
- 検証を繰り返した

## 研究概要

Use this order:

1. Research theme in plain language.
2. Why the research matters.
3. Method or approach.
4. Difficulty or originality.
5. Result, current progress, or expected contribution.
6. Skills gained that connect to work.

For non-specialist recruiters, avoid dense technical terms unless the user asks for a technical version.

## Interview Follow-Up

For each ES answer, prepare likely follow-up questions:

- なぜその会社でなければならないのか
- その経験で最も苦労したことは何か
- 周囲と意見が対立した時どうしたか
- 失敗から何を学んだか
- 入社後にどのように活かせるか
